Made for Drupal Devs by Drupal Devs.

Founded in 2011 by Chris McGrath, Drupal Developer / Front-end Architect and serial entrepreneur — our platform was created to be a place for Drupal resources to connect with employers and network with one another on contract project opportunities. Over time our resource base has grown to over 250+ developers worldwide.

In 2017 Albert Volkman, a Drupal Developer / Back-end Architect and top 100 Drupal contributor, joined the team as a partner. The two collaboratively guide the technical vision of the product.

Both partners are committed to and active in the Drupal community — continually evangelizing the platform with a goal of connecting resources with quality projects and growth opportunities. Chris is a co-coordinator of the Olympia WA DUG and the Drupal Group on Linkedin.

You will often find each speaking at local user groups, camps and cons on technical or career related topics. The company is of course a Drupal Association member, and regular sponsor of DrupalCon.
